Our Mission

The Land of the Free Foundation is committed to celebrating the men and women of the Armed Forces of the United States, past, present and future. The foundation will support programs that help our armed service personnel and their families and will ensure that the commitment of our nation's heroes will never be taken for granted and that all Americans recognize, because of the sacrifices of our armed service personnel, we will always live in the "Land of the Free".




Ways to Give

We are committed to providing the financial resources necessary to help our active duty personnel and their families, and our veterans who are transitioning back into civilian life after their years of loyal service, but we need your help.

Whether you Donate Now, participate in Land of the Free Foundation fundraising events, or Shop to Support our armed service personnel and their families, every dollar counts. Please join us in funding the programs that can change the lives of those in need. And, we’ll make sure the monies you donate reaches them.
